SVR SPORTS ACADEMY


Had a dream to play for the Nation?? then come and join us to make the process easier and to fulfil your wish.SVR Sports Academy believes that sports today play a vital role in development of a human being and achieving excellence in sports has a great bearing on national prestige and morale. In order to meet the increasing demands of the changing scenario, National as well as International Government has taken it upon themselves to implement programs to promote excellence in sports. Chennai sports Academy is an effort to attain this vision. Reach your goals and inspire your best self. At Chennai Sports Academy, emphasis is laid on scholastic excellence coupled with sports techniques and character building leading to success in life.
SVR sports Academy has been started by our Chairman Dr.S.Vivek Reddy garu in order to give every ambitious student a chance to play for his nation and to make his/her parents proud of them. The Secret of our success is that we only choose the students based on their capability to play instead of their Financial Background. Our main aim is to give this country as many great players as possible.
we Have started as a small coaching center and now we've spread across the north and south india Including major cities and minor towns. All we expect from our coaches and students is one thing and that is their co-operation to get a step towards the success. We have total of 396 branches which are widely spread across the india. we encourage all the sports and games starting from minor to major.
